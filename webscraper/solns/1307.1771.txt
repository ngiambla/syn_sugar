in 1980, gizatullin classified rational surfaces endowed with an automorphismwhose action on the neron-severi group is parabolic: these surfaces are endowedwith an elliptic fibration invariant by the automorphism. the aim of thisexpository paper is to present for non-experts the details of gizatullin'soriginal proof, and to provide an introduction to a recent paper by cantat anddolgachev.
