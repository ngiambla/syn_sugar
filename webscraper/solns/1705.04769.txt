blockchain technology as a whole is experiencing a dramatic rise in adoption,in no small part due to the developer-friendly ethereum network. while thenumber of smart-contract powered distributed applications (dapps) continues torise, they face many of the same challenges all new technologies face as theyare introduced to a market. by modeling the consumer adoption of blockchaintechnology and analyzing scholarly literature on supply-side factors affectingthe diffusion of technology, we seek to prove the growth of a dapp can beaccelerated using abstraction, whole product planning, and complementaries.
