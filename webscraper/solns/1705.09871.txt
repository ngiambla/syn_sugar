rfid tags are small electronic devices that can be used to identify objectsand people. the present paper presents an rfid-based integrated system that hasbeen developed to allow the identification and traceability of products andsubsets in whatever activity field. thus this system offers to the users thepossibility to define their own information format to be stored in thetransponder. the potential beneficiaries of this system are companies thatactivate in fields that lend themselves admirably to rfid technologies.
