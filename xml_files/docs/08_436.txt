
 
1 NuEra Wellness Centre Pty Ltd (the company) was charged under s 155(5)(a) of the Trade Practices Act 1974 (Cth) (the Act) of refusing or failing to comply with a notice issued to it under s 155(1) of the Act.
The offence took place on 22 November 2006.
The company entered a plea of guilty to the offence on 22 December 2007.
2 This offence is a reflection of the conduct of Micheal Rana, sole director of the company at the time of the offence, but under the influence of Paul Rana.
3 The circumstances of Micheal Rana's wrongdoing are set out in the reasons for judgment in Australian Competition and Consumer Commission v Rana [2008] FCA 435.
The wrongdoing is of a serious order and on this basis the appropriate penalty is a fine of $6000.
I certify that the preceding three (3) numbered paragraphs are a true copy of the Reasons for Judgment herein of the Honourable Justice North.
