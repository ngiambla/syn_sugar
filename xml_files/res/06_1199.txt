 The respondent is guilty of contempt of court in that in breach of order 4 made by Wilcox J on 15 December 2005 he had from no later than 19 January 2006 up to 1 March 2006 failed to change his registered business name to 'Simply Natural Floor Coverings' (with 'floor coverings' as two words).
 The respondent is guilty of contempt of court in that in breach of order 4 made by Wilcox J on 15 December 2005 he had from 19 January 2006 up to 2 March 2006 used the business name 'Simply Natural Floorcoverings' (with 'floorcoverings' as one word) on his website.
