 The first related to Effem's claim that Wandella's mark should not be registered because it is substantially identical with or deceptively similar to registered trade marks owned by Effem (SCHMACKOS and DOGS GO WACKO FOR SCHMACKOS) which concern similar goods (s 44(1) of the Act).
 Plainly, as Effem submitted, there is a substantial similarity between Wandella's mark and a central word (WACKO) in Effem's registered mark, notwithstanding the inclusion of an H in Wandella's mark and a concluding S.
