 INTRODUCTION 1 The first respondent has objected to the competency of an appeal filed by the appellant against a decision of a Federal Magistrate given on 26 April 2007.
 The Federal Magistrates Court dismissed this application on 29 April 2005 and the appellant was ordered to pay the first respondent's costs fixed in the amount of $6,300.
 The Magistrate summed up her reasons by saying: "In essence the applicant wishes to challenge the merits of the Tribunal's decision which is not open to this court...The Tribunal made findings of fact uniquely within its jurisdiction and such findings of fact were supported by probative material before it.
