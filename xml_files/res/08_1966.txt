 The Federal Magistrate's commentary about the similarity in capitalisation of the website names is also not significant as web addresses are not capital-sensitive. 14 Significantly, I cannot see that his Honour made any finding about whether the words which comprise JEM's web address had acquired a secondary distinctive meaning through extensive use.
