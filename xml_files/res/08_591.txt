 Insofar as the Non-Propagation Agreements contain a term that purports to give Fleming's Nurseries the right, upon breach, to remove and/or destroy the Sweetheart and Black Star cherry trees for which he paid, the term constitutes a penalty and is unenforceable.
 If the trial is not split, it would be necessary for the applicants and the respondents to call additional evidence to deal with both damages and account of profits, and to do so would involve considerable time and expense.
