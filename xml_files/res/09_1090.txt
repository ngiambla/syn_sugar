 These types of losses may be characterised as "concrete" (i.e. loss of life or personal injury and loss of or damage to property) and "abstract" (i.e. financial loss resulting from a "concrete" loss).
 However, consequential damage --- physical damage as well as abstract damage --- arising out of loss or damage in the concrete sense shall give rise to limitation of liability as well as to a maritime lien.
