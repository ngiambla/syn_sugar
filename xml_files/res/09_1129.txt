 The intervener, the Commissioner, is appointed pursuant to s 9 of the BCII Act, with functions under s 10 of the BCII Act of: (a) monitoring and promoting compliance with the Act; (b) investigating suspected contraventions by building industry participants of the Act; and (c) instituting, or intervening in, proceedings in accordance with the Act.
 The Commissioner is taken to be a party to the proceeding and has all the rights, duties and liabilities of such a party under the provisions of s 71(2) of the BCII Act.
