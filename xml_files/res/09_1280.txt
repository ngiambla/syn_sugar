 In that application, two of the applicants (the third was later joined) sought the issuing of mandatory orders against the AFP, the Commonwealth Government and the Immigration Department.
 The Court has jurisdiction, under s 39B of the Judiciary Act 1903 (Cth) ("the Judiciary Act "), to deal with a proceeding in which the writ of mandamus is sought.
 Under s 39B(1) the Court has jurisdiction "with respect to any matter in which a writ of mandamus ... is sought against an officer or officers of the Commonwealth" (emphasis added).
