 Section 37 of the Act provides that "a person is eligible for an invalidity service pension if the person is a veteran and has rendered qualifying service and is permanently incapacitated for work in accordance with a determination under section 37AA": s 37(1)(c).
 Section 37AA obligates the respondent to specify by written determination the circumstances in which persons are permanently incapacitated for work for the purposes of s 37(1)(c) of the Act.
