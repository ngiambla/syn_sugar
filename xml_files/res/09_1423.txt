 The Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA) applies for the time for service and the hearing of the motion to be abridged and for orders that, in accordance with item 6(1) of sch 4 to the Australian Communications and Media Authority (Consequential and Transitional Provisions) Act 2005 (Cth) (the ACMA Act), ACMA be substituted for the Australian Communications Authority (ACA) as the applicant in the proceeding with that substitution to take effect from 1 July of 2005.
 In the statement of claim, it was pleaded that the applicant was established in the manner I have indicated and that from 1 July 2005 the Australian Communications Authority would be substituted by the Australian Communications and Media Authority as the applicant by operation of item 6(1) of Sch 4 of the ACMA Act.
