 Contentions Playspace contended that Toy Shed incurred the debt with it at various stages between August and December 2004, when the playground equipment was delivered to Toy Shed in Darwin and it issued invoices for the supply of the equipment.
 It follows from this that the substance and commercial reality of the situation meant that, once these packages of playground equipment were dispatched to Toy Shed, it became obligated to pay for them.
 On the other hand, the other indicators for which Ms Wright was able to locate relevant documentation, allowed her to express the opinion Toy Shed was solvent, at least until early 2005.
