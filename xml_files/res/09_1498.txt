 The original Malanbarra Yidinji People's application was lodged with the National Native Title Tribunal (the Tribunal) on 21 August 1994 and an application by George Davis on behalf of the descendants of Jimmy Longdon, members of the Dulabed Aboriginal Corporation, was lodged with the Tribunal on 23 February 1995.
 Power of the Court Pursuant to s 13 of the Act, applications for the determination of native title may be made to the Federal Court in relation to areas for which there is no approved determination of native title.
