 The appellant claimed before the MRT that her command of her mother language was inadequate, that she would have difficulty adapting to the Korean education system and she would lack the support of friends.
 The MRT assessed her claim to have exceptional reasons in the following way: The Tribunal does not accept that the applicant has established exceptional reasons for the grant of the subclass 573 visa.
 She stated that it would be difficult to adapt to a different educational system in South Korea where she would not have the support of friends as she would have in Australia.
