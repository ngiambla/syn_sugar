 Accordingly, the principal orders which are sought at this hearing are orders under section 411(1) of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) ("the Act"), that the plaintiff convene a meeting of members of the company and an order approving the explanatory statement for distribution to shareholders.
 The scheme is an acquisition scheme between the plaintiff, People Telecom Limited ("People"), and its members, under which all of the issued capital of People will be acquired by M2 Telecommunications Group Ltd ("M2") via its subsidiary M2 Telecommunications Pty Ltd.
 The first is that there is a break fee of up to $300,000 payable by People in the circumstances set forth in the Scheme Booklet.
 The proxy commitments are not given for consideration, nor are they given to representatives of M2 or its subsidiary which is to be the acquirer.
