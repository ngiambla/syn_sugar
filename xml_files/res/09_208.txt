 It is not considered that, in the circumstances of the present application, the Court has any power to set aside the Order as previously made or --- even if there is power --- that the Order should be set aside.
 In DJL v The Central Authority [2000] HCA 17 ; (2000) 201 CLR 226 , the High Court held that the Full Court of the Family Court did not have power to re-open final orders after their entry.
