 However, since the enactment of s 424AA and s 424A(2A), the Tribunal does not have to comply with s 424A(1) if the Tribunal proceeds in accordance with s 424A(2A).
 Section 424A(2A) is engaged "if the Tribunal gives clear particulars of the information to the applicant, and invites the applicant to comment on or respond to the information, under s 424AA".
