 The Vaysmans, Victoria Vaysman and Samba Enterprises Pty Ltd contend that there is, at least, a reasonable prospect that they will be successful in establishing that the trade mark should be cancelled by order of the Court.
 The six Respondents also raise an issue which is common to their defences of the copyright claim and the causes of action in passing off and contraventions of the Fair Trading Act and the Trade Practices Act .
 The Vaysmans' submissions seem to go even further and admit the possibility of the defence being available in respect of representations of the sun device and the logo on boxes, care tags and information booklets.
