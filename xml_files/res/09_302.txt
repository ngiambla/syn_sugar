 The Tribunal was required to determine, as at 19 November 2004, the percentage of Mr House's incapacity in relation to civil employment, and the appropriate classification in respect of that incapacity for the purposes of s 30 and s 34 of the Act.
 Accordingly, the Tribunal concluded that the personality disorder of Mr House was one cause of his retirement, and that for the purposes of s 34(1B)(a) of the Act, alcoholism and the personality disorder of Mr House were his prescribed physical and mental impairments (at [99]-[100]).
