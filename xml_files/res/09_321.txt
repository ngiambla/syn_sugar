 I do not accept that the applicant was ever involved in farmers' protests in relation to land issues or in any other anti-government protests or appeals in China nor that there is a real chance that he will be involved in any such activities if he returns to China now.
 CONCLUSION For these reasons, I consider the Federal Magistrate erred in failing to detect that the third Tribunal had failed to conduct its further review of the delegate's decision according to law, by failing to consider and take into account the jurisdictional error identified by Jacobson J in his reasons for judgment.
