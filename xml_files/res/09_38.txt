 Midas Australia operates, directly or through franchisees, a network of car care centres across Australia providing various automotive repair services, with an established client base of about 900,000 active customers.
 Mr Georges deposed that, overall, the landlords with whom he has had contact to date have been supportive of his proposal to sell the business as a going concern.
 In his affidavit, Mr Georges deposed that, despite the administrators' best efforts, they have been unable to complete all the investigations that they consider are necessary and that they believe will affect the creditors' decision on the post-administration future of the companies.
 The administrators expect that they will be able to ascertain, within the next two months, whether a sale of the business may be achieved and what, if any, surplus may be available to unsecured creditors.
