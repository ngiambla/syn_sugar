 On 19 August 2008, the applicant moved for judgment consequent upon what was said to be an agreement reached during the mediation before a Deputy District Registrar of this Court.
 The solicitor who then represented the applicant held a different understanding from that held by the solicitor for the respondents as to the terms of the agreement reached at the mediation.
 ADMISSIONS In the course of the hearing on 19 August 2008, I pointed out to the parties the threshold difficulty in my inquiring as to anything which transpired in the course of the confidential mediation.
 THE MOTION On 10 February 2009, the applicant requested that the Court enter judgment against the respondents for $17,500 --- the sum in respect of which the concession was made.
