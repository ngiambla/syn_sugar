 In Burgess the applicant submitted that, as the whole of the assets of the company had been misappropriated, the income that had been derived and was included in the assessable income for the relevant year, must have been part of that which was misappropriated.
 The Commissioner puts the argument in terms that once the money in respect of which the loss is suffered loses its character as income it cannot be identified as the money included in the taxpayer's assessable income.
