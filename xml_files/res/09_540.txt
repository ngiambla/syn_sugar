 The respondent represented to Environment Australia on or about 26 June 2003 that the additional conditions were: ... specifically relevant to the lungfish that would, in the opinion of Burnett Water Pty Ltd and its expert advisers, be appropriate to protect the lungfish and mitigate damage to the lungfish from the construction and operation of the Burnett River Dam.
 When the DEWR audit team conducted a site inspection of the dam on 26 June 2007, SunWater staff acting as agents for the respondent operated the upstream fishway to represent to the auditors that the upstream fishway was in fact operational.
