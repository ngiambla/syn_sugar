 Section 24A of the ACC Act provides that an examiner (being a person appointed under s 46B(1)) may conduct an examination for the purposes of a "special ACC operation/investigation" (defined by s 4(1) by reference to determinations of the Board).
 Each person who is: (a) a member of the staff of the ACC; and (b) identified in writing by the CEO, or a delegate of the CEO, as a person whose duties include providing services in relation to ACC operations and investigations.
