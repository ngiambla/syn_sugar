 Mr Pick replied that he would bring the barge down to the wharf and secure it to a structure with rope so that it would remain stable.
 Mr Clarke considered that Mr Van Efferen was performing his duties satisfactorily and did not accept that complaints which Mr Pick attributed to John Holland had substance.
 It was not suggested to Mr Van Efferen that it was likely that he would have resigned and gone elsewhere prior to the completion of the project.
