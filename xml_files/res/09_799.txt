 Indeed, "Kookaburra" was the winning entry in that competition which had, as one of the rules for entry, a condition that:- All matter entered to become the property of the Guide Association.
 The composers and EMI rely upon two signed or initialled manuscripts of "Kookaburra" submitted by Ms Sinclair as her entry in the competition to satisfy the statutory requirement that an assignment be signed by the copyright owner.
 The documentary evidence shows that Ms Sinclair was a strong supporter of the Victorian Girl Guides movement and there are a number of competing inferences that are open as to how she came to enter "Kookaburra" in the competition.
