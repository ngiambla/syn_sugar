whether personal service of each document required
applicability of reg 16.01 of bankruptcy regulations to service of each class of document. held : reg 16.01 applied to bankruptcy notice but not to creditor's petition, which must be served personally. whether requirement of personal service can be waived. held : yes
and it was waived by debtor's participating in hearing and doing other acts without challenging service.
