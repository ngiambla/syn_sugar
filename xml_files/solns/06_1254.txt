winding up
application by asic to wind up a publicly listed company (and its subsidiary) on the ground of insolvency, the just and equitable ground and contended conduct by directors in the affairs of the company in their own interests rather than members as a whole (ss 461(1)(e) and (k), s 459p, s 459a, and s 462(2) of the corporations act 2001 (cth)).
