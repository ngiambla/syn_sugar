appeal from orders made by federal magistrate dismissing application for review of decision of refugee review tribunal affirming decision of minister's delegate not to grant appellant protection visa
where appellant invited to attend hearing of tribunal
where appellant indicated that she did not wish to attend hearing
where appellant claimed that she later informed tribunal that she had changed her mind and wished to attend hearing
whether federal magistrate erred in rejecting evidence of appellant
whether federal magistrate erred in refusing to find that tribunal fell into error by proceeding without affording appellant hearing
whether federal magistrate erred in refusing to find that tribunal committed jurisdictional error
held, no error in reasoning of federal magistrate
appeal dismissed.
