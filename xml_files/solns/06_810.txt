referral from registrar of application under s 459p of corporations act 2001 (cth) for order that company be wound up in insolvency under s 459p
where company already subject of creditors' voluntary winding up
where applicant also sought order terminating voluntary winding up under s 482
whether registrar has power to make order under s 482
whether order under s 482 necessary before making order under s 459a
reasons for making order under s 459a in such circumstances
held, order under s 482 not necessary before making order under s 459a
registrar's lack of power to make order under s 482 therefore no obstacle to registrar making order under s 459a
in circumstances, no good reason to make order under s 459a
application dismissed.
