cartel conduct
visy companies and amcor companies held 90 per cent of the corrugated fibreboard packaging market in australia
between january 2000 and october 2004 visy and amcor engaged in price-fixing and market sharing contrary to s 45 of trade practices act 1974 (cth)
amcor received conditional immunity from action by the australian competition and consumer commission
commission brought action against visy companies and three of its senior officers four categories of contraventions: over-arching understanding in 2000 to maintain market share and increase prices; annual price increase understandings each year for 2000 to 2003, customer price understandings and compensation understandings
respondents admitted liability
parties submitted an agreed statement of facts
