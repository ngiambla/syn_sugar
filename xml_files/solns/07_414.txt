aggrieved applicants seek injunction restraining native title registrar from removing entry relating to claim from the register of native title claims
whether s 190(4)(d) of the native title act 1993 (cth) has the effect that the registrar must remove entry once proceeding "dismissed" following trial, or only if and when dismissal sustained upon appeal
held : the former, so that there was no scope for interference with native title registrar's proposed removal of entry from the register following dismissal at trial.
