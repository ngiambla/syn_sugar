whether centrelink was right to deduct retirement benefits paid to the appellant by the united states social security administration from his newstart allowance pursuant to schedule 13 of the social security (international agreements) act 1999 (cth)
whether the appellant's united states pension is exempt as it is superannuation
definition of the term "benefit" in schedule 13
"benefit" comprehends a united states social security benefit
appellant's benefit was not paid under any of the australian superannuation guarantee laws specified in schedule 13
appeal dismissed.
