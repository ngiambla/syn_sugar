application for review of decision of refugee review tribunal
whether tribunal erred in failing to provide appellant with particulars of information relating to a document provided to the appellant in a s 424a letter
court must determine objectively what the tribunal considered was the reason or part of the reason for affirming the decision under review
s 424a of the migration act 1958 (cth) cannot be interpreted as imposing an obligation not found to exist on the proper construction of that section
appeal dismissed.
